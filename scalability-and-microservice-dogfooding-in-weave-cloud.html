#madScalability

Scalability and microservice dogfooding in Weave Cloud

by Alfonso Acosta (Software engineer at Weave.works)

cloud.weave.works is a fast and simple way to visualize, manage and monitor containers and microservices. In this talk we will go through its architecture and performance challenges, including a high level microservice breakdown and some obscure details in our datastructures and Golang's runtime.
